nano /etc/network/interfaces
#vi /etc/network/interfaces
配置多IP：
auto eth0  
iface eth0 inet static
address 
netmask 255.255.255.0
gateway 
dns-nameserver DNS服务
        
auto eth0:0  
iface eth0:0 inet static  
address   
netmask 255.255.255.0

auto eth0:1 
iface eth0:1 inet static  
address   
netmask 255.255.255.0

按Ctrl+x退出，然后按Y
/etc/init.d/networking restart
重启生效
